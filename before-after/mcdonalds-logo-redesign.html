McDonald’s Logo Redesign

⇄ just drag this slider below and compare the design before and after.

The McDonald’s fast-food empire was created in 1954 on the legendary Route 66. Now it’s a cafe museum, accessible to tourists on tours. The story began as a small place for motorists. The white letters of McDonald’s today proudly adorn 36,000 restaurants around the world.

Where did it all begin? The story begins in a town in San Bernardino, California, where the McDonald brothers Richard and Maurice (Dick and Mac) moved in the 1940s.

The McDonald’s logo is symbolic of the arches that were the substance of the newly-constructed architecture of the first franchised restaurant in 1952.
